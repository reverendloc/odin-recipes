Chermoula

Description

Moroccan dressing

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 C fresh cilantro leaves
  • 8 garlic cloves
  • 1 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 6 Tbs lemon juice (2 lemons)
  • 3/4 C extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Pulse cilantro, garlic, cumin, paprika, cayenne, and salt in blender until chopped. ~ 10 pulses.
  2. Add lemon juice and pulse to combine.
  3. Transfer to a medium bowl and slowly whisk in oil.
  4. Cover and let sut 30 min to mix.
