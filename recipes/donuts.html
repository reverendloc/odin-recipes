Donuts

Description

My dad's donut recipe. I still need to make this, then will report back!
Makes 2 dozen 3" donuts

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs (separated)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 Tbs shortening (or butter?)
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk or sour milk
  • 3 1/2 cups sifted flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp sals
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon

Steps

  1. Beat thoroughly: 4 egg yolks OR 2 whole eggs
  2. Beat in 1 cup sugar + 2 tbs shortening or butter
  3. Stir in 3/4 cup buttermilk
  4. Sift together and stir in dry ingredients
  5. Turn onto a generously floured board. Roll our 1/3 inch thick
  6. Let rest 20 min
  7. Cut with donut cutter
  8. Fry in hot oil until brown, turning half way through cooking
  9. Drain on paper towels
  10. Serve plain or roll in cinnamon/sugar or sugar or glaze
