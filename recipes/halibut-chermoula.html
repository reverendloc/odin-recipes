Halibut with Carrots, White Beans, and Chermoula

Description

Instant Pot recipe for Halibut in a tasty sauce with veggies. Last time we cooked this we needed to remove the halibut and finish it in the toaster oven.

Ingredients

  • 1 shallot, sliced thin
  • 1 Tb extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 lb carrots, peeled
  • 1 15-oz can navy beans, rinsed
  • 1/2 C chicken or veg broth
  • 4 (6-oz) skinless halibut filets, 1 1/2" thick
  • 3 Tb Chermoula
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1 C fresh parsley or cilantro
  • 2 Tbs sliced almonds, toasted

Steps

  1. Combine shallot, oil, and lemon juice in medium bowl; set aside.
  2. Cut carrots into 2 inch lengths. Leave thin pieces whole, halve medium, quarter large.
  3. Combine carrot, beans, and broth in Instant Pot
  4. Fold sheet of aluminum foil into 16"x16" sling. Brush Halibut with 1 Tb Chermoula and sprinkle with salt. Arrange halibut skinned side down into sling. Using sling, lower into Instant Pot on top of carrot mixture. Narrow edges of sling should be resting on sides of Instant Pot insert.
  5. Lock lid into place and close pressure release. Cook on High for 0 minutes. Once cooked, immediately quick-release pressure
  6. Fold marshmallow mixture into whipped cream/pineapple mixture
  7. Transfer fish to large plate, tent with foil, and let rest (or finish in toaster oven if needed). Stir remaining 2 Tb Chermoula into carrot mixture and season with s&p.
  8. Add parsley and almonds to bowl with shallot mixture and gently toss to combine. Season with s&p. Top fish with parsley salad.
