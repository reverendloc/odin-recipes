Judy Salad

Description

The myth, the legend. Some day we'll try a veg version, but here's the classic.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 lb mini marshmallows
  • 1 package Lemon Jello
  • 2 cups whipping cream
  • 1 can crushed or chunk pineapple
  • 1 small jar Marachino cherries
  • 1-2 Graham crackers

Steps

  1. Bring cup milk to a boil
  2. Add 1 poung marshmallows and 3 tablespoons of Lemon Jello
  3. Take off heat, mix and cool (don't melt smooth)
  4. Whip 2 cups cream
  5. Add 1 can pineapple (drained)
  6. Fold marshmallow mixture into whipped cream/pineapple mixture
  7. Top with 1 small jar marashino cherries (drained)
  8. Top with graham cracker crumbs
  9. Chill
